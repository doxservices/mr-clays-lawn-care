New lawns, installed right.

A new development doesn't come with a finished yard. We install lawns from the ground up, planned around your soil, drainage, and sunlight, and establish the grass in stages until it's fully grown in.

✓
Site and soil assessment first. We test soil composition, grading, and drainage before anything is planted, so the plan fits your yard instead of a generic template.
✓
Seeding matched to your conditions. Grass and seed selection, soil preparation, and planting are chosen for your yard's soil type, sun exposure, and the season.
✓
Establishment care until it's grown in. Scheduled watering, fertilization, and first cuts take the lawn from bare soil to full, even coverage.
✓
A specific plan for every yard. Each new-lawn project gets its own written plan and quote based on the site assessment, starting with a simple consultation.

Simple, transparent pricing.

Our services are priced with a USD reference so your rate stays fair and predictable, and the current JMD rate is always shown clearly for local payment.

Current standard rate

The standard monthly lawn maintenance plan is J$15,000 per month for two visits, based on a US$50 per visit reference rate.

Why we use a USD reference

The USD reference keeps pricing consistent when exchange rates move. The JMD amount is shown for your convenience and is only reviewed if the exchange rate changes materially.

How you pay

Payment is arranged directly with us in JMD, with no automatic billing, no hidden fees, and no long-term lock-in. You always know your rate before any work begins.

Rate changes with notice

If a rate adjustment is ever needed, you'll be told in advance with the new amount clearly stated. Your service agreement always spells out the exact terms.
